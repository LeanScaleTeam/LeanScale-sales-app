# QFlow

## Overview

QFlow AI is a comprehensive go-to-market finance platform leveraging AI to power revenue planning and analysis, helping companies improve alignment between go-to-market and finance teams.

## Features

- **Data dictionary:** Provides a common language for go-to-market and finance teams
- **Revenue waterfall:** Tracks deal progress through the sales pipeline
- **Rolling forecast:** Enables future planning capabilities
- **Scenario planner:** Allows testing different scenarios and impact analysis on revenue
- **Generative AI assistant:** Delivers insights and recommendations

## Benefits

- Improved alignment between go-to-market and finance teams
- Increased forecast accuracy
- Optimized go-to-market efficiency
- Saved time
- Improved decision-making
- Addressed potential problems before they become major issues

## Pricing

QFlow AI offers multiple pricing plans designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes.
