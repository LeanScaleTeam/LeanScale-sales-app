What's a Claybook?
A Claybook is a pre-built Clay workflow (Table) that solves a specific GTM use case. Each Claybook includes: enrichment waterfall configuration, scoring logic, CRM field mapping, integration setup, documentation, and handoff training.


The 10 Claybooks
1. Market Map Claybook — $7,500
What it does: Builds your complete ICP/TAM database in Clay, enriches every account, scores for fit, assigns tiers, and pushes to CRM with valuations.

Complexity: High (foundational, everything else builds on this)

What's included:

ICP definition workshop (firmographic + technographic criteria)
Master account Clay table with full enrichment waterfall
Up to 5 data sources (Apollo, Clearbit, ZoomInfo, + 2 vertical-specific)
ICP fit scoring with weighted criteria
3-tier account classification (T1/T2/T3)
Account valuation methodology
CRM custom fields and bi-directional sync
TAM visualization setup
Documentation + 90-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 50,000 accounts

Estimated hours: 40-60

Prerequisites: None (this is the foundation)

Typical buyer: Series A+ building GTM infrastructure


2. Persona Mapping Claybook — $4,000
What it does: Adds contact-level enrichment to your Market Map. Identifies key personas at target accounts, enriches contacts, scores for relevance, and syncs to CRM.

Complexity: Medium

What's included:

Persona definition workshop (titles, seniority, function)
Contact Clay table linked to account table
Contact enrichment waterfall (up to 4 sources)
Persona fit scoring
Contact tier assignment (aligned to account tiers)
CRM contact field mapping and sync
Documentation + 60-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 100,000 contacts

Estimated hours: 20-30

Prerequisites: Market Map Claybook (or equivalent existing setup)

Typical buyer: Teams ready to operationalize outbound at scale


3. Automated Inbound Enrichment Claybook — $5,000
What it does: Enriches inbound leads in real-time as they enter CRM. Filters junk, deduplicates against existing accounts, scores, tiers, and routes.

Complexity: Medium

What's included:

Inbound trigger configuration (form fills, demo requests, trials)
Pre-enrichment filters (personal emails, below-threshold companies, out-of-geo)
Deduplication logic against existing Market Map
Enrichment waterfall (up to 4 sources)
ICP scoring and tier assignment
CRM field updates and lead routing triggers
Credit optimization (skip enrichment for known accounts)
Documentation + 60-min handoff session

Scope: Unlimited inbound volume

Estimated hours: 15-25 (post-Market Map); 30-40 (standalone)

Prerequisites: Market Map recommended (saves 80% build time and credits)

Typical buyer: Companies with growing inbound motion


4. Automated Outbound Claybook — $6,000
What it does: Builds targeted outbound lists from your Market Map, enriches contacts, personalizes at scale, and pushes to sales engagement platform.

Complexity: Medium-High

What's included:

Outbound targeting criteria definition
Account selection logic from Market Map
Contact discovery and enrichment
Personalization data points (company news, tech stack, triggers)
Sales engagement platform integration (Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo, Amplemarket)
Sequence/cadence field mapping
Credit monitoring and budget controls
Documentation + 60-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 10,000 contacts/month

Estimated hours: 25-35

Prerequisites: Market Map Claybook required

Typical buyer: Teams scaling BDR outbound with Clay + SEP


5. Lead Scoring Claybook — $3,500
What it does: Implements ICP-driven lead scoring in Clay, combining firmographic fit with enriched data signals, then syncs scores to CRM/MAP.

Complexity: Medium

What's included:

Scoring criteria workshop
Fit score calculation (firmographic + technographic)
Behavioral signal integration (if available)
Score normalization and threshold definition
MQL threshold configuration
CRM/MAP score field sync
Score decay/refresh logic
Documentation + 45-min handoff session

Scope: Applied to inbound and/or existing database

Estimated hours: 15-25

Prerequisites: ICP definition (can be part of this engagement)

Typical buyer: Companies formalizing lead qualification


6. ABM Target Account Enrichment Claybook — $5,500
What it does: Takes your named account list, deeply enriches each account and contact, adds intent signals, and creates ABM-ready segments in CRM.

Complexity: Medium-High

What's included:

Named account list ingestion (up to 500 accounts)
Deep account enrichment (extended firmographics, technographics, news, funding)
Buying committee mapping (identify key personas per account)
Contact enrichment for buying committee
Intent signal integration (if 6sense/Demandbase/Bombora available)
Account tier and engagement priority scoring
CRM segment creation for ABM campaigns
Documentation + 60-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 500 named accounts, 5 contacts per account

Estimated hours: 25-35

Prerequisites: Named account list; Market Map helpful but not required

Typical buyer: Companies running target account selling motion


7. CRM Data Cleanup Claybook — $3,000
What it does: Enriches and standardizes existing CRM data. Fills gaps, corrects errors, normalizes fields, and flags duplicates.

Complexity: Low-Medium

What's included:

Data audit and gap analysis
Enrichment waterfall for missing fields
Field standardization (industry, company size, geo)
Duplicate flagging (not full dedup—separate project)
Data quality scoring
Batch update to CRM
Before/after data quality report
Documentation + 45-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 25,000 records

Estimated hours: 15-20

Prerequisites: CRM access

Typical buyer: Companies with dirty CRM data blocking other initiatives


8. Customer Segmentation Claybook — $4,500
What it does: Enriches your customer base with firmographic and technographic data, then segments for CS coverage models, expansion targeting, and retention analysis.

Complexity: Medium

What's included:

Customer data export and enrichment
Firmographic and technographic enrichment
Segmentation criteria workshop (industry, size, tech stack, ARR tier)
Segment assignment logic
Health/expansion signal identification
CRM customer segment fields
CS platform integration (if applicable)
Documentation + 60-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 10,000 customers

Estimated hours: 20-30

Prerequisites: Customer list with company identifiers

Typical buyer: CS teams building coverage models or expansion plays


9. Event Lead Enrichment Claybook — $2,500
What it does: Takes event lead lists, enriches and scores against ICP, deduplicates against CRM, and routes hot leads for immediate follow-up.

Complexity: Low-Medium

What's included:

Event lead list ingestion template
Enrichment waterfall (standard sources)
ICP scoring and tier assignment
Deduplication against existing CRM records
Hot lead flagging and routing triggers
CRM lead creation with event attribution
Reusable for future events
Documentation + 30-min handoff session

Scope: Up to 5,000 leads per event

Estimated hours: 10-15

Prerequisites: Event lead list; ICP definition

Typical buyer: Marketing teams running events/trade shows


10. Signal-Based Prospecting Claybook — $6,500
What it does: Monitors intent signals (hiring, funding, tech changes, news) across your TAM and surfaces accounts showing buying signals for prioritized outreach.

Complexity: High

What's included:

Signal source integration (job postings, funding alerts, news, tech installs)
Signal-to-ICP mapping (which signals matter for your buyers)
Trigger logic configuration
Account alerting and prioritization
Contact discovery for triggered accounts
Sales engagement platform push
Weekly signal digest reporting
Documentation + 60-min handoff session

Scope: Monitoring up to 25,000 accounts

Estimated hours: 30-40

Prerequisites: Market Map Claybook required

Typical buyer: Teams moving to intent-based outbound

When to Sell Claybooks vs. Retainer
Sell Claybooks when:
Single, well-defined use case
Budget under $20k
Need delivery in 2-4 weeks
Customer new to Clay / needs quick win
Clay partnership referral (DQ lead)
Sell Retainer when:
Multiple GTM initiatives beyond Clay
Ongoing strategic guidance needed
Scope unclear / discovery required
Budget supports 40+ hours/month
Change management support needed
