# Funnel Analytics

The Funnel Analytics Dashboard focuses on analyzing conversion performance across the sales pipeline using several key components.

## Key Conversion Rates

Displays conversion rates from MQL to SAL, SAL to SQL, and SQL to Closed Won, providing a year-to-date blended view across segments.

## Top Performing Lead Sources

This section visualizes lead source performance based on conversion rates and revenue generation:

| Lead Source | Conversion Rate | Revenue Output |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Sales Outbound | 25% | Significant |
| Paid Advertising | Higher revenue | Lower conversion |
| SDR Outbound | 14% | Lower revenue |

## Comparative Analysis

Compares high and low-performing lead sources to identify improvement opportunities, such as contrasting Sales Outbound versus SDR Outbound effectiveness.

## Comprehensive Breakdown

Applies the same analytical approach to other funnel stages:

- MQL to SQL conversion analysis
- SAL to SQL conversion analysis
- Performance across different timeframes

## Final Thoughts

Leverage the dashboard pack for data-driven decision-making to enhance business outcomes. Understanding funnel performance at each stage allows teams to identify bottlenecks and optimize the customer acquisition process.
