## AI Agent Recommendations

**Stage-appropriate approach:** AI at Platform stage is embedded in processes, governed for compliance and quality, and deployed for both efficiency gains and strategic analysis. AI governance is essential.

## AI Agent Use Cases

| Category | Use Cases | Business Value |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Sales Automation** | Email drafting, call prep, CRM updates | Rep productivity +20-30% |
| **Sales Intelligence** | Deal scoring, next-best-action, risk detection | Win rate improvement |
| **Marketing Automation** | Content generation, personalization, A/B testing | Scale without headcount |
| **CS Automation** | Health monitoring, intervention triggers, renewal prep | CSM capacity +30-50% |
| **RevOps Automation** | Data hygiene, reporting, forecasting assist | Ops efficiency |
| **Executive Intelligence** | Board prep, competitive analysis, market trends | Strategic speed |
| **M&A Support** | Due diligence research, integration planning | Deal velocity |

## AI Governance

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| **Use Case Approval** | Review process for new AI deployments |
| **Data Access** | Clear rules on what data AI can access |
| **Quality Assurance** | Human review for customer-facing AI outputs |
| **Compliance** | Alignment with data privacy regs (GDPR, CCPA) |
| **Vendor Vetting** | Security review for AI tools and APIs |
| **Model Monitoring** | Track accuracy, drift, edge cases |

## Human-AI Handoff

| Scenario | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| **First Draft** | AI generates, human reviews and refines |
| **Escalation** | AI handles routine, human handles complex |
| **Quality Gate** | AI assists, human approves before send |
| **Analysis** | AI synthesizes, human interprets and decides |

## Platform-Stage AI Stack

| Category | Tools | Purpose |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **Conversation Intelligence** | Gong AI, Chorus | Call analysis, coaching |
| **Sales Assistant** | Clari, People.ai | Activity capture, forecasting |
| **Writing Assistant** | Jasper, Writer | Content generation |
| **CS AI** | Gainsight AI, ChurnZero AI | Health prediction, intervention |
| **General AI** | OpenAI, Anthropic APIs | Custom use cases |

> **What NOT to do at this stage:**
>
> - **AI without governance** — ungoverned AI creates compliance and quality risk
> - **AI replacing judgment** — AI should augment, not replace, human decision-making
> - **Ignoring AI security** — customer data flowing to AI tools needs security review
