## Milestone 1: ABM / ABS Process and System - 1. Strategy & Account Development
**Tag:** `abm-abs`
**Description:** Define ABM strategy, build target account list, map buying committees, and select ABM platform
**Hours:** 62h

### Task List: (ABM / ABS) 1. Assessment & ICP Development
**Contains:** Parts 1-2

| Task | Est | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| 1. Audit Current Account Targeting Approach | 2.5h | Evaluate how accounts are currently identified, prioritized, and engaged across sales and marketing. End state: Gap analysis document showing current state vs. desired ABM/ABS capabilities.<br /><br />• Interview sales leadership on current account selection criteria and prioritization methods<br />• Review marketing's current targeting approach (spray-and-pray vs. focused)<br />• Pull data on win rates by account segment/size over last 12 months<br />• Document current tech stack capabilities for account-based targeting<br />• Identify gaps between current state and ABM best practices<br />• Quantify the opportunity cost of misaligned targeting (e.g., "60% of closed-won came from 15% of accounts pursued") |
| 2. Define ABM Program Goals and Success Metrics | 2h | Establish clear objectives, KPIs, and success criteria for the ABM/ABS initiative. End state: Documented program goals with measurable targets approved by leadership.<br /><br />• Define primary objective (pipeline growth, deal size increase, conversion rate improvement)<br />• Set specific KPIs: target account engagement rate, pipeline from target accounts, ACV targets<br />• Establish baseline metrics from current performance<br />• Define leading indicators (account engagement scores, buying committee coverage)<br />• Define lagging indicators (pipeline generated, deals closed, revenue from target accounts)<br />• Get leadership sign-off on goals and 6-12 month timeline expectations |
| 3. Secure Cross-Functional Alignment and Buy-In | 2h | Align sales, marketing, and leadership on ABM strategy, roles, and commitments. End state: Executive sponsorship confirmed and cross-functional team committed to ABM motion.<br /><br />• Present ABM business case to leadership (ROI data: 87% of B2B marketers report ABM outperforms other investments)<br />• Define roles: Marketing owns campaigns, Sales owns account relationships, RevOps owns data/process<br />• Establish governance model and meeting cadence (weekly ABM syncs)<br />• Create SLA between marketing and sales on account handoffs and follow-up timing<br />• Document decision rights for account prioritization and resource allocation |
| 4. Develop Ideal Customer Profile Framework | 3.5h | Create a data-driven ICP framework that identifies companies most likely to buy and succeed with the product. End state: Documented ICP with firmographic, technographic, and behavioral criteria weighted by importance.<br /><br />• Analyze closed-won deals from last 24 months for common characteristics<br />• Identify firmographic criteria: industry, employee count, revenue range, geography<br />• Identify technographic criteria: current tech stack, tools used, platform preferences<br />• Identify behavioral signals: growth stage, funding events, hiring patterns<br />• Weight criteria by correlation to deal success and lifetime value<br />• Create ICP scoring model (1-100 scale)<br />• Validate ICP with sales team input on "best fit" accounts |
| 5. Build Initial Target Account List | 3h | Generate prioritized list of target accounts using ICP criteria and available data sources. End state: Tiered target account list with 50-500 accounts ready for enrichment.<br /><br />• Pull potential accounts from CRM that match ICP criteria<br />• Supplement with accounts from data providers (ZoomInfo, Apollo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator)<br />• Apply ICP scoring model to rank accounts<br />• Create tiering structure (Tier 1: 10-50 accounts for 1:1, Tier 2: 50-200 for 1:few, Tier 3: 200-500 for 1:many)<br />• Remove accounts with active opportunities or recent losses (quarantine period)<br />• Validate list with sales leadership for strategic fit and relationship history |
| 6. Enrich Target Account Data | 2.5h | Enhance target account records with additional firmographic, technographic, and contact data. End state: Target accounts enriched with complete company profiles and initial contact lists.<br /><br />• Enrich firmographic data: exact employee count, revenue, locations, subsidiaries<br />• Add technographic data: current tools, platforms, tech stack (using tools like BuiltWith, HG Insights)<br />• Identify intent signals from third-party providers<br />• Append key contacts from each account (minimum 3-5 per account)<br />• Update CRM records with enriched data<br />• Flag data gaps requiring manual research |

### Task List: (ABM / ABS) 2. Buying Committees & Platform Integration
**Contains:** Parts 3-4

| Task | Est | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| 7. Define Buying Committee Roles and Personas | 3h | Create persona definitions for each role in the typical buying committee. End state: Documented persona profiles with pain points, motivations, and messaging angles for each role.<br /><br />• Identify typical buying committee roles: Economic Buyer, Technical Evaluator, Champion, End User, Blocker<br />• Analyze past deals to understand who was involved at each stage<br />• Create persona profiles with demographics, responsibilities, KPIs, and pain points<br />• Define messaging themes that resonate with each persona<br />• Map content types preferred by each persona (executives want ROI, technical want specs)<br />• Document objections typically raised by each persona type |
| 8. Map Buying Committees for Target Accounts | 4h | Identify and document the specific individuals in buying committee roles for each target account. End state: Buying committee maps for Tier 1 and Tier 2 accounts with contact information.<br /><br />• For Tier 1 accounts: Research and identify specific individuals for each buying committee role<br />• Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo, Apollo to find contacts<br />• Document each contact's role, likely influence level, and known relationships<br />• Identify gaps in buying committee coverage (missing roles)<br />• Add contacts to CRM and tag with persona/role classifications<br />• Create account org charts for Tier 1 accounts showing decision-making structure |
| 9. Develop Engagement Sequence by Persona | 3.5h | Create tailored engagement playbooks for each persona type. End state: Documented engagement sequences with touchpoints, messaging, and content mapped by persona.<br /><br />• Define touchpoint sequence for each persona (email, LinkedIn, phone, ads)<br />• Map content to each stage: awareness content for early stage, ROI content for decision stage<br />• Create messaging templates tailored to each persona's pain points<br />• Define handoff triggers between marketing and sales engagement<br />• Document "do not contact" rules and opt-out handling<br />• Build sequences in sales engagement platform (Outreach, Salesloft) |
| 10. Evaluate ABM Platform Options | 3h | Compare ABM platform options against client requirements, tech stack compatibility, and budget. End state: Platform recommendation with feature comparison and pricing approved.<br /><br />• Document requirements: intent data, advertising, personalization, analytics, integrations needed<br />• Evaluate leading platforms: 6sense, Demandbase, Terminus, RollWorks, Triblio<br />• Compare on: intent data quality, CRM integration depth, ad platform capabilities, pricing model<br />• Request demos from top 2-3 vendors<br />• Check references from similar-sized B2B SaaS companies<br />• Present recommendation with pros/cons and total cost of ownership<br />• Get budget approval and initiate procurement |
| 11. Implement ABM Platform Core Configuration | 3.5h | Set up ABM platform account and configure core settings. End state: ABM platform operational with CRM connected and basic configuration complete.<br /><br />• Create ABM platform account and configure admin settings<br />• Connect to CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot) via native integration or API<br />• Configure data sync: accounts, contacts, opportunities, activities<br />• Set up user roles and permissions for marketing and sales users<br />• Configure account matching rules to link platform data to CRM accounts<br />• Verify bidirectional data flow between platform and CRM |
| 12. Configure Intent Data and Account Scoring | 3.5h | Set up intent data tracking and account scoring within the ABM platform. End state: Intent signals flowing and account scores calculated based on engagement and intent.<br /><br />• Define intent topics relevant to product/solution (keywords, competitors, pain points)<br />• Configure third-party intent data feeds within platform<br />• Set up first-party intent tracking (website visits, content engagement)<br />• Create account scoring model combining intent signals and engagement data<br />• Define score thresholds for "surging" accounts requiring immediate attention<br />• Configure alerts to notify sales when accounts hit score thresholds<br />• Test scoring with known active accounts to validate model |
