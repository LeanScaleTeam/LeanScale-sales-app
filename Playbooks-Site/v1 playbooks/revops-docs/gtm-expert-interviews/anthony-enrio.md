## Overview

This interview features Anthony Enrio, a RevOps leader turned founder, discussing GTM strategy in the AI era, the evolving role of RevOps, and practical tools for early-stage startups.

## Key Themes

### Revenue per FTE
- Understanding and optimizing revenue efficiency
- Benchmarking against industry standards
- Building lean, high-performing teams

### From "Growth at All Costs" to Efficient GTM
- The shift in startup mentality post-2022
- Building repeatable, scalable processes
- Focusing on unit economics from day one

### Balancing AI and Human Input
- Where AI adds the most value in GTM
- Tasks that still require human judgment
- Building AI-augmented workflows

## Takeaways

- Early-stage startups should focus on revenue per FTE as a key metric
- Efficient GTM requires repeatable processes before scaling
- AI tools can accelerate RevOps but require human oversight for strategy
