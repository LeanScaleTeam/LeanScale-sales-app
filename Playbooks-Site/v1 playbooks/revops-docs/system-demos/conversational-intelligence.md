# Conversational Intelligence

## Overview

This documentation section focuses on tools that help teams extract and analyze customer communications throughout the sales process. These platforms capture insights from customer interactions and conversations to inform sales strategy.

## Key Tools

The page highlights two primary solutions in this category:

**Gong** - A platform designed to extract comprehensive data from customer communications and interactions.

**Unthread** - A tool that leverages communication platforms like Slack and Microsoft Teams to surface customer insights and engagement patterns.

## Purpose

Conversational intelligence solutions serve a critical function in modern revenue operations by enabling teams to:

- Monitor what customers are thinking during sales engagements
- Track customer sentiment after interactions
- Extract actionable intelligence from sales calls and conversations
- Analyze communication patterns to improve sales effectiveness
