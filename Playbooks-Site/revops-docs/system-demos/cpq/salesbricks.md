# Salesbricks - CPQ Software Documentation

## Introduction

Salesbricks is a comprehensive CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) platform designed for B2B sales teams. It offers an intuitive interface and automates back-office processes to streamline the quoting and deal-closing workflow.

## Key Features

- **Seamless Configuration**: Sellers can build proposals and quotes with a "Shopify-like interface" for ease of use

- **Back-Office Automation**: The platform handles revenue recognition, billing, and reporting tasks automatically

- **Enhanced Collaboration**: Enables efficient back-and-forth communication with customers for revisions and edits

- **Real-Time Tracking**: Sellers monitor deal progress and access crucial metrics instantly

- **Diverse Product Support**: Accommodates usage-based products, user licenses, and platform access models

- **Time-Saving Efficiency**: Significantly reduces time required to create and send proposals, contracts, and invoices

- **Comprehensive Pipeline Visibility**: Sales teams gain clear insights into their sales pipeline for better forecasting

- **Customer Relationship Management**: Centralizes contact information, purchase history, and support tickets

## Benefits

- **Accelerated Deal Closure**: Streamlines the proposal, contract, and invoice generation process for faster deal completion

- **Enhanced Pipeline Management**: Real-time visibility allows sales teams to optimize strategies and identify improvement areas

- **Streamlined CRM**: Seamlessly manages customer relationships to improve service and foster long-term growth

- **Robust Customization**: Businesses can tailor the platform to align with unique processes and needs

- **User-Friendly Interface**: Designed for simplicity, ensuring a smooth learning curve for sales teams
